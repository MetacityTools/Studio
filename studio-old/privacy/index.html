Privacy Policy

for Metacity Studio

Metacity Studio is a front-end only application with no backend or server-side components. We do not collect any personal data, use telemetry, or track user activity. We prioritize data security and have implemented measures to protect the limited data we process. For more detailed information, please read the following Privacy Policy.

Effective Date: 1.6.2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Metacity Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the Metacity Studio website (studio.metacity.cc) ("Website") and the Metacity Studio application ("Application"). By accessing or using the Website or Application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described in this Privacy Policy, you should not use the Website or Application.

Information Collection

We want to assure you that Metacity Studio does not collect any personal data or personally identifiable information when you use our Website or Application. We designed Metacity Studio to be a front-end only application with zero backend and no server-side components. Therefore, we do not request or store any personal information such as your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

Usage Data and Telemetry

Metacity Studio does not utilize any telemetry or analytics tools to collect data on how the Application is used. We do not collect information about the features you use, actions you take, or any other usage statistics.

Cookies

Metacity Studio does not use any cookies or similar tracking technologies on the Website or within the Application. We do not store any information on your device that would be retrieved during subsequent visits.

Third-Party Services

Metacity Studio does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data or track user activity. Our Application operates solely on the client-side, without any reliance on external services.

While Metacity Studio currently does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data or track user activity, we are considering the possibility of introducing integration with Google Sheets and Google Login in the future. It's important to note that even if we implement these integrations, we will not collect or store any data through them. Any data exchange with Google Sheets will be solely for the purpose of providing you with seamless access to your own data, and any use of Google Login will be limited to authentication purposes. We are committed to maintaining the same high level of privacy and data protection standards, and we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly if and when these integrations are implemented. Rest assured that we will never access, use, or store any of your personal data without your explicit consent.

Data Security

Although we do not collect any personal information, we take data security seriously. We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data that we do process. However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of the information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the revised Privacy Policy on the Website. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at metacity@stdio.cz.
